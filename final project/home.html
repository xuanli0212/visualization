This project aims to explore the data of unidentified flying object (UFO) sightings in the United States using effective visualization techniques. The dataset examined in this project are extracted from the database of National UFO Reporting Center (NUFORC), and we are focusing on data from 2004 to 2014.This system is divided into four major sections, aiming to anwer the four questions below: a) Where - Geographical information of sightings b) When - Date and time of the sightings events. c) What - The shape of the UFOs (d) How - Additional information revealed in people’s depiction of the events.Please feel free to explore different visualizations by clicking on the buttons on the left.
